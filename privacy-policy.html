Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Nostalgia Memorial ("we", "our", or "the app") is a geospatial messaging application that lets users leave messages tied to real-world locations. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Authentication data: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a unique identifier and, if you choose to share it, your email address. We do not receive or store your Apple ID or Google password.
  • Profile information: Your chosen display name (nickname) and optional avatar photo.

Location Data

  • Precise location: The app uses your device's GPS to show nearby memorials and to let you place new ones. Location data is sent to our server only when actively using the app to search for or create memorials. We do not track your location in the background.

User-Generated Content

  • Memorials and messages: Text content you create, along with the geographic coordinates where you placed them.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • To display your profile to other users
  • To show memorials near your current location
  • To place your memorials at the correct geographic position
  • To enforce our community guidelines (processing reports and blocks)

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Account and content data is stored on our secured servers.
  • Avatar images are stored using Cloudflare R2 cloud storage.
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using the iOS Keychain.
  • All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The only third-party services involved are:

  • Apple and Google: For sign-in authentication only. Their respective privacy policies apply to their sign-in services.
  • Cloudflare: For secure storage of avatar images.

5. Your Content and Other Users

Memorials and messages you create are visible to other users of the app who are near the location where you placed them. Your display name and avatar are shown alongside your content.

If you block another user, their content will be hidden from you and your content will be hidden from them.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen in the app.
  • When you delete your account, your personal data (profile information, email, avatar) is permanently removed from our servers.
  • Messages you created will remain visible but will be attributed to "Deleted User" and will no longer be linked to your identity.

7. Children's Privacy

Nostalgia Memorial is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
